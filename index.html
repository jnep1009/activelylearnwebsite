Reading in depth

With Actively Learn, teachers help students to reach depth in reading through Activate, Support and Reveal (ASR) thinking.

Activate

Thinking deeply needs to be activated by providing a purpose, asking questions to stop and reflect, and motivating peer discussions. With Actively Learn, you help students engage and collaborate more, which helps them persevere through a complex text

Support

Many factors can derail students from achieving depth in their reading. Actively Learn amplifies instructional guidance to help overcome insufficient background knowledge, vocabulary gaps, disfluency, and more

Reveal

Targeted and effective feedback depends on teachers' ability to see how students are thinking as they construct knowledge. Teachers can see students' writing (both annotations and question responses), proficiency and vocabulary challenges—all in real time
