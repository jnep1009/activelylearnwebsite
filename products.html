Benefits

With instruction embedded in the text, the focus of class time becomes discussion.

Because Actively Learn enables teachers to embed explanatory content and questions directly in the text, teachers find that the focus of class time changes once they transition to digital reading.

Instruction is informed and guided by student questions, comments, and performance data.

With Actively Learn, teachers walk into class knowing what their students thought as they read. They’ve seen their students’ “I don’t understand” flags, their responses to questions, and their annotations. They’ve also seen where students concentrated their discussion threads and the conversations that have already begun prior to class.

Teachers can differentiate instruction based on data.

The data from Actively Learn shows which students are struggling and gives actionable insights into the areas that students need help with. These teachers can then use extra help notes to give those particular students the support they need, while omitting it for those who don’t.

Actively Learn encourages deeper thinking and increases student engagement.

Actively Learn takes care of comprehension and task completion by monitoring student activity and allowing teachers to insert instruction in the margins. This leads to increased engagement in the material and a more dynamic academic environment.
