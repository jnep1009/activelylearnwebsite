Reading in Depth

Through Activate Support and Reveal (ASR) thinking, students learn how to read in depth and analyze the text.

  • Learn More

Activate

Thinking deeply needs to be activated by providing a purpose, asking questions to stop and reflect, and motivating peer discussions. With Actively Learn, you help students engage and collaborate more, which helps them persevere through a complex text.

Next

Support

Many factors can derail students from achieving depth in their reading. Actively Learn amplifies instructional guidance to help overcome insufficient background knowledge, vocabulary gaps, disfluency, and more.

Next

Reveal

Targeted and effective feedback depends on teachers' ability to see how students are thinking as they construct knowledge. To help students become expert readers, teachers can see students' writing (both annotations and question responses), their effort, their proficiency, their vocabulary challenges, and their struggles—all in real time.
